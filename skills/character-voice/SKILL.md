# Character Voice

## Description

Use this skill to differentiate how characters speak, ensure dialogue matches personality and background, and flag lines that feel interchangeable or out of character.

## Workflow

1. Review dialogue samples per character.
2. Identify repeated phrasing, flat cadence, or mixed voice signals.
3. Suggest voice markers, vocabulary shifts, and revision examples.
4. Preserve clarity while increasing distinction.
